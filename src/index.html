About Us

valgrind was founded in September 2014 as a collaborative space for hackers of all backgrounds, skills, and ages. Operating internationally and independently of any institution, valgrind creates an environment where passionately curious individuals can learn together and grow their skills. We prioritize voluntary participation and maintaining an engaging, learning-focused atmosphere.
